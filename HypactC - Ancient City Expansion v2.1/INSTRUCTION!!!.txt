To get the random spawns working, just follow these quick steps:

Open: MythicMobs/randomspawns/hpc_ancientcity_expansion
Locate the Worlds option
Replace it with the exact name of your server world Folder

Edit the Chance/s the Mobs can Spawn, just try and figure out what fits best, but 0.5 should be good. 

You can also edit the Condition "mobsinradius" just Change the blockradius (r=32) to (r=48) for more free space between the same mobs!

That’s it, once updated, everything should work as intended!
